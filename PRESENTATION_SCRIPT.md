### 🔥 FINAL DELIVERY TIPS for Recording:
* **Pacing:** Speak slightly fast, but enunciate clearly.
* **Keep it moving:** No over-explaining. If there is dead air, move the mouse.
* **Smooth mouse movement:** Don't jerk the cursor across the screen. Glide it deliberately to where you want the viewer's eye to go.
* **Energy:** Maintain a consistent, confident energy loop all the way to the end! You built something awesome here.
